Message Tags

Attach #hashtags to your messages to easily group them on Telegram! You can add/delete tags, and drag and drop to reorder.

  • 8 tags max.
  • Must be unique.
  • At least 2 characters long.
  • Up to 30 characters long.
  • Number and letters only.

Activity Logs

All message and activity logs are stored locally on your browser and not shared with anywhere else. No logs will be recorded in Incognito Mode.
